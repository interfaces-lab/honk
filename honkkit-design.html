Policy

Keep the system small

  • Use semantic roles before literal values or local color-mix formulas.
  • Add feature-owned variables only in the feature stylesheet, and alias shared roles when possible.
  • Do not introduce near-duplicate shadow tiers; use the ladder above.
  • Expose new reusable tokens through `packages/app/src/index.css` only after they exist in `tokens.css`.
