Sidebar update button designs

Footer treatments and lifecycle patterns for the desktop update CTA. Designs 1–5 explore stacked layouts; 5 is the shipped compact chip row. Designs 6–15 extend the footer row. Designs 16–32 apply UX research: non-intrusive awareness, explicit consent, state persistence, progress feedback, and semver urgency tiers.

UX principles (from Electron/desktop update guides): least-disruptive pattern that works; persist dismiss/snooze per version; opt-in before download; show progress during transfer; reserve blocking UI for mandatory/security updates; surface What's New when workflow changes; always offer retry on failure.

Urgency tiers (semver)

Escalate prominence by semver diff. Patch = accent chip; minor = warm warning; major = blocking overlay (not shown here). Linux may fall back to external link.

Bugfix / patch release. Current shipped chip weight.
Default. No workflow interruption expected.
